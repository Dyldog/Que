
# Que 

An iOS app to help me learn the Spanish interrogative words.

After pressing "Start", the app shows one of the words (listed below) in English or Spanish in large type. There is a stopwatch timer ticking upwards at the top of the screen. Tapping the screen shows the translation of the word (to Spanish or English, respectively) and very large buttons for answering if I got it correct or incorrect. I should then be made to wait for a period of time we'll call the wait time. The wait time is double my total time or double the existing wait time (whichever is bigger) when I get it wrong. The wait time is half my total time when I get it right. My "total time" is the wait time plus the amount of time it takes me to answer after the wait time ends. After the wait time, I should be shown another word, and the process repeats.

## Words 

| Spanish | English |
|-|-|
| Como | How? |
| Dónde | Where? |
| Quién | Who? (singular) |
| Quiénes | Who? (plural) |
| Qué | What? |
| Cuál | Which? (singular) |
| Cuál | Which? (plural) |
| Por qué | Why? |
| Cuánto | How much? |
| Cuántos | How many? (male) |
| Cuántas | How many? (female) |
| Cuándo | When? |
